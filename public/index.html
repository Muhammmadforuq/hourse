about us

We have more than 20+ years riding experience

Born on November 18, 1976, in Madrid and currently residing in Olivares, Sevilla, José Antonio Olea Rodríguez has emerged as a prominent figure in the world of equestrianism and horse training. With over three decades of dedication and experience, he is recognized as one of the foremost horse trainers on both the national and international stages.

Professional Experience

20+ Years of Professional Equestrian Experience

José Antonio is not only a consummate rider but also a sports technical specialist in equestrianism and a territorial judge in working equitation. His education in Administrative Vocational Training and his innate ability to understand horses have granted him a comprehensive approach to equine training and horse training.

He has participated in various competitions such as Western dressage, classical dressage, cutting, morphological contests, high school, and working equitation, always standing out for his skill and unique connection with horses.

Love Horses ?

Presently, José Antonio Olea Rodríguez manages an impressive riding school with 60 horses in Salteras. This space serves not only as a training center but also as a place for horse boarding and lessons. With two training arenas, it offers an optimal environment for the development and training of both riders and horses.
