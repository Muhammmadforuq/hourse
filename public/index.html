Professional Experience

20+ Years of Professional Equestrian Experience

José Antonio is not only a consummate rider but also a sports technical specialist in equestrianism and a territorial judge in working equitation. His education in Administrative Vocational Training and his innate ability to understand horses have granted him a comprehensive approach to equine training and horse training.

Philosophy and Ethical Treatment of Horses What sets José Antonio apart is not just his technical skill but also his profound respect and connection with horses. His ethical approach to horse training is reflected in the exceptional treatment he provides to his equine pupils. His empathy and understanding of the individual needs of each horse have earned him recognition as a trainer who seeks not only performance but also the well-being and happiness of his four-legged companions.

Contributions and Teaching José Antonio's passion extends beyond competition. He has contributed to the equestrian world by conducting courses in Western dressage and teaching at the municipal riding school. Moreover, he has shared his extensive knowledge through specialized clinics, with a recent focus on working equitation.

Current Facilities Presently, José Antonio Olea Rodríguez manages an impressive riding school with 60 horses in Salteras. This space serves not only as a training center but also as a place for horse boarding and lessons. With two training arenas, it offers an optimal environment for the development and training of both riders and horses.
